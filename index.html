Cheatsheet

Pattern
space-separated strokes/notes — each one is a beat. A track loops at its own length, seamlessly. | bar lines are cosmetic.
Rests & sustain
- rest   ~ hold previous
harmonium, sarangi, esraj & bansuri sustain for the note's written length — stretch with ~; plucked strings ring out naturally
Subdivide a beat
[ti ra ki ta]   [Sa Sa'] — nestable
Tabla bols
dha dhin na tin ta tun ge ke ka te ti ra ki kat
Sargam notes
Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni
komal: lowercase re ga dha ni · tivra: Ma+
octaves: Sa' up, Sa, down
Key (Sa)
moves the whole ensemble — the tabla re-tunes to ring on the same Sa, like matching the lehra to the drum
vol & oct
vol 0–2 per track · oct shifts a melodic track by whole octaves
Live coding
Edit while it plays, then Cmd/Ctrl+Enter — the loop never stops, new notes land in phase.
